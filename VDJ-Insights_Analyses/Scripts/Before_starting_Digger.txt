
Digger has motifs for humans and rhesus macaque IG.
Motifs for rhesus macaque TR and gorilla need to be downloaded

Go to ~/.local/lib/python3.10/site-packages/digger/motifs/

Make the designated folders:
In folder rhesus_macaque: TRA, TRB, TRD and TRG
For the gorilla make a folder: gorilla
      and make supfolders: IGH, IGK, IGL, TRA, TRB, TRD and TRG


####Rhesus Macaque####
cd ~/.local/lib/python3.10/site-packages/digger/motifs/rhesus_macaque

For the macaque in the correct new folders:
parse_imgt_annotations "https://www.imgt.org/ligmdb/view?format=IMGT&id=IMGT000076" TRA/IMGT000076_genes.csv TRA
parse_imgt_annotations "https://www.imgt.org/ligmdb/view?format=IMGT&id=IMGT000076" TRD/IMGT000076_genes.csv TRD
parse_imgt_annotations "https://www.imgt.org/ligmdb/view?format=IMGT&id=IMGT000073" TRB/IMGT000073_genes.csv TRB
parse_imgt_annotations "https://www.imgt.org/ligmdb/view?format=IMGT&id=IMGT000059" TRG/IMGT000059_genes.csv TRG

cd TRA
calc_motifs TRA IMGT000076_genes.csv

cd ../TRD
calc_motifs TRD IMGT000076_genes.csv
#These annotations do not consist of D segments for this reason: (I made a note in my motif files to remember this for the long run.)
cp ../../human/TRB/*D* .

cd ../TRB
calc_motifs TRB IMGT000073_genes.csv
#These annotations do not consist of D segments for this reason:
cp ../../human/TRB/*D* .

cd ../TRG
calc_motifs TRG IMGT000059_genes.csv
